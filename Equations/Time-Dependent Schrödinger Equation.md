$$
i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \Psi(r,t) = \left[ - \frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \nabla ^2 + V(r, t)  \right] \Psi(r, t)
$$
or 
$$
i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \Psi(r, t) = \hat{H} \Psi(r, t) \quad \text{with} \quad \hat{H} = -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\nabla ^2 + V(r, t)
$$

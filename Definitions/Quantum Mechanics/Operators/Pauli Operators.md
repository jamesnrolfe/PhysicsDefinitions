The Pauli [[Operators|operators]] are represented by the following matrices 
$$
\hat{X} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}, \quad \hat{Y}= \begin{pmatrix}
0 & -i \\
i & 0
\end{pmatrix}, \quad \hat{Z} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

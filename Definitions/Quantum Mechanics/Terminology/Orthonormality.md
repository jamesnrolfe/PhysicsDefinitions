Two states are **orthonormal** if they are [[Orthogonality|orthogonal]] *and* [[Normalisation|normalised]]. 

If a set of states $\{ \ket{\psi_{i}} \}$ forms an **orthonormal basis**, then
$$
\braket{ \psi _{i} | \psi_{j} } = \delta_{ij}.
$$

### Spherical Coordinates
To transfer from **Cartesian coordinates** to **Spherical/Polar coordinates**, use
$$
\begin{align}
x &= r \cos \theta \sin \phi \\
y &= r\sin \theta \sin \phi \\
z & = r\cos \phi
\end{align}
$$
